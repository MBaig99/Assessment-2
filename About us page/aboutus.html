Who We Are

Cat Haven NGO is a passionate team full of animal lovers dedicated to rescuing, sheltering, and finding forever homes for cats in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe, nurturing environment for abandoned and stray cats while educating communities on proper animal care and the importance of adoption.
